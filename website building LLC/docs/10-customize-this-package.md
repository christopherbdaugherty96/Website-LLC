# Customize This Package Before Launch

This starter kit is intentionally broad enough to work for a new U.S.-based solo founder. Before you use it in the real world, customize these items.

## Business Identity

- Your business name
- Your state
- Your entity type
- Your business address
- Your phone number
- Your email
- Your domain

## Offer Details

- Your niche
- Your service area
- Your actual package names
- Your actual starting prices
- Your deposit terms
- Your revision limits
- Your maintenance plan details

## Legal and Financial Details

- Governing law state in the agreement
- Your real invoicing details
- Your late-fee policy
- Your tax process
- Your local business license requirements
- Your final attorney-reviewed contract language

## Website Details

- Replace the placeholder business name in the agency site
- Replace the placeholder phone and contact form details
- Add your real call to action
- Add 1 to 3 sample projects
- Add your actual niche examples

## Outreach Details

- Your sender name
- Your cold email signature
- Your real pricing language
- Your lead sheet or CRM
- Your opt-out process
- Your do-not-contact list process

## Strongly Recommended Before First Client

- Have a local attorney review the service agreement
- Have a CPA confirm your tax setup
- Confirm state registration and license requirements
- Decide whether clients will own hosting directly or through a documented managed setup
