# Simple Financial Model

## Startup Costs

Lean version:

- domain: $10 to $30 per year
- email: $6 to $20 per month
- hosting or demo hosting: $10 to $40 per month
- design or dev tools: $0 to $80 per month
- bookkeeping software: $0 to $35 per month
- CRM or lead tracking: $0 to $30 per month
- insurance: varies
- business filing fees: state-dependent

## Example Revenue Scenario

Scenario A:

- 2 starter sites per month at $1,000 = $2,000
- 1 growth site per month at $2,000 = $2,000
- 5 maintenance clients at $99 = $495

Monthly gross revenue:

- $4,495

Scenario B:

- 2 growth sites per month at $2,000 = $4,000
- 1 premium site every other month at $4,000 = about $2,000 monthly average
- 10 maintenance clients at $149 = $1,490

Monthly gross revenue:

- about $7,490

## First-Year Target Model

Reasonable early target:

- 12 to 24 builds in year one
- 6 to 12 maintenance clients by year end

## Recommended Money Rules

- collect a deposit before work starts,
- do not wait until launch to bill the entire project,
- save a percentage of every payment for taxes,
- keep software costs light until revenue is consistent,
- avoid long custom projects early.

## Break-Even Thinking

If your monthly fixed costs are low, even one closed site per month can keep the business moving while you build pipeline and referrals.

## Best Early KPI Dashboard

Track these weekly:

- leads added,
- outreach sent,
- replies,
- calls booked,
- proposals sent,
- close rate,
- average project value,
- maintenance clients,
- monthly recurring revenue,
- cash collected.
