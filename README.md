# Y.O.U. — Your Own Vision

Modern, action-focused websites for local businesses that deserve to be seen, trusted, and chosen online.

## Live Website
https://christopherbdaugherty96.github.io/Website-LLC-Y.O.U.-You-re-Own-Vision-/

## Core Idea
Y.O.U. (Your Own Vision) helps small local businesses improve weak, outdated, or missing online presence.

The goal is simple: help a real business look trustworthy online and make it easier for customers to call, book, visit, order, or request a quote.

Y.O.U. is built around one belief:

> A good local business should not lose customers because it looks invisible, outdated, or unfinished online.

## Who This Helps
Y.O.U. is focused on practical websites for local service and walk-in businesses, including:

- restaurants and diners
- lawn care and landscaping businesses
- barbershops
- salons
- cleaning services
- contractors and roofers
- auto detailers
- mobile service providers
- small businesses relying only on Facebook, Google listings, or word of mouth

## What Y.O.U. Builds
- starter business websites
- redesigns for outdated sites
- landing pages
- restaurant and menu sites
- quote-request pages
- booking/contact-focused pages
- portfolio demo concepts for outreach
- local visibility and first-impression upgrades
