# Outreach Packet

Last updated: 2026-04-26

## Purpose

A fast-use outreach guide for contacting local businesses without overthinking each message.

## Core Rule

Personalized beats spam. Short beats long. Helpful beats pushy.

## Message Template 1 — Website Refresh

Hi [Name], I took a quick look at your online presence and noticed a few easy wins that could make it easier for customers to trust you and contact you. I build practical websites for local businesses. If helpful, I can show you a quick example direction.

## Message Template 2 — No Website / Weak Presence

Hi [Name], I noticed your business could benefit from a cleaner online presence so customers can quickly see what you offer and how to reach you. I help local businesses with simple websites built to generate calls, bookings, and quote requests.

## Message Template 3 — Referral Intro

Hi [Name], I was referred your way because your business may benefit from a stronger website or easier customer inquiry flow. Happy to share ideas if useful.

## Follow-Up 1

Just following up in case this is helpful. Happy to show a simple example or quick improvement ideas.

## Follow-Up 2

Wanted to send one last follow-up. If improving your website or online presence becomes a priority later, feel free to reach out.

## Quick Qualification Questions

- Do you already have a website?
- What do you most want more of: calls, bookings, quote requests, trust, cleaner branding?
- Do you need a refresh or a brand new website?
- What timeline matters most?
- Do you have a rough budget in mind?

## Rule

Do not mass-send generic messages. Use the right message for the right business.
