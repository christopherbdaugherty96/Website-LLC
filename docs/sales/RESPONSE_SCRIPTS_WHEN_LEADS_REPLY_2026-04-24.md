# Response Scripts When Leads Reply

Date: 2026-04-24

Copy and paste as needed.

---

## 1. Interested

Awesome, I appreciate the reply. I’d love to learn a little about your business and what you’d want the website to help with most—more calls, more quotes, better presentation, easier booking, etc.

From there I can show you the best direction and a simple option to get started.

---

## 2. How much?

It depends on what you need, but most projects start around:

- Website Refresh: $250
- Basic Website: $500+
- Standard Website: $1,000+
- Premium / Custom: Quote based

If you tell me a little about your business and goals, I can point you to the best fit.

---

## 3. Not right now

No problem at all, I understand. When the timing is better, I’d be happy to help.

Would it be okay if I checked back in down the road? I can also send you a simple idea now so you have it whenever you're ready.

---

## 4. We already have a website

That makes sense. A lot of businesses already have something in place.

What I often help with is improving what’s already there—better first impressions, easier customer action, stronger mobile experience, and clearer calls or quote requests.

If you'd like, I can share a few ideas based on your current setup.

---

## 5. Tell me more

Absolutely. I build websites for local businesses designed around one goal: helping more customers choose you and contact you.

That usually means clearer information, stronger trust, better mobile experience, and an easier path to call, book, or request a quote.

What kind of business do you run, and what would you most want improved?

---

## 6. Follow-up / No Reply

Hi, just following up in case this got buried. If improving your online presence is still something you’d like to explore, I’d be happy to help or share a simple idea for your business.

---

## 7. How long does it take?

Most projects can get a first version ready quickly once I have the basic info from you.

Smaller refreshes can move faster, while larger custom builds take longer. If you tell me what you need, I can give you a realistic timeline.

---

## 8. What do you need from me?

Usually just a few basics to get started:

- Business name
- Services offered
- Best phone/email
- Photos or logo (if you have them)
- Any style ideas you like

From there I can handle the build and keep it simple for you.
