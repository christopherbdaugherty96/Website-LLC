# Sales & Referral Program

## Purpose
Reward people who help Y.O.U. connect with real businesses that become paying website clients.

## Sales Partner Commissions
- Basic Website: $100
- Standard Website: $250
- Professional Website: $400

## Performance Bonuses
- 5 completed sales: +$150 bonus
- 10 completed sales: +$500 bonus

## Referral Reward
- $100 for each completed paid website project from a qualified referral.

## When Payout Happens
A payout happens only when:
1. The business agrees to move forward
2. Payment or deposit is received
3. The project officially begins

## What Does Not Qualify
No payout for:
- unpaid leads
- duplicate referrals
- cancellations
- verbal interest only
- businesses already in pipeline
- fake or unresponsive leads

## Best Prospect Types
- restaurants
- lawn care companies
- barbershops
- salons
- contractors
- cleaning services
- auto detailers

## Partner Mindset
The goal is not random names. The goal is real conversations with business owners who need help online.
