# Restaurant Website Business Plan

## Why This Document Exists
Restaurants remain a strong niche because owners quickly understand the value of better menus, easier contact, mobile usability, and stronger first impressions.

This document now serves as a restaurant-specific lane inside the larger Y.O.U. business model.

## Core Restaurant Offer
- Mobile-first website
- Menu / hours / map / call CTA
- Better branding and visuals
- Basic SEO structure
- Optional updates and maintenance

## Pricing Direction
- Starter: $500+
- Growth: $750-$1,000+
- Premium: $1,250+
- Monthly support: $50-$300

## Why Restaurants Convert Well
- Customers search fast
- Decisions are visual
- Directions and calling matter immediately
- Menus are often outdated or hard to use
- Owners can feel lost business quickly

## Target Restaurant Types
- diners
- family restaurants
- fast casual spots
- upscale independents
- themed restaurants
- takeout-focused local spots

## Example Priority Queue
1. Bryan's Neighborhood Coney
2. Brody's On Cass
3. Walt's Original Coney Island
4. Slate Eatery
5. Bourbon Street Gumbo and PoBoys

## Execution Standard
Every restaurant demo should include:
- clear hero section
- menu visibility
- one-tap call button
- map/directions
- hours
- trust visuals
- mobile-first flow

## Strategic Reminder
Restaurants are important, but Y.O.U. should not become restaurant-only. Use restaurant wins to fund expansion into other niches.
