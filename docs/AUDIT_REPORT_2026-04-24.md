Date: 2026-04-24  
Updated: 2026-04-26

## Executive Summary

Auralis Digital has moved beyond the idea stage. The business now has a live public website, clear pricing direction, early portfolio/demo direction, outreach planning, and a stronger value proposition for Michigan local businesses.

The main remaining gaps are execution-focused: portfolio depth, real lead capture, repo documentation, testimonials, and consistent outreach.

## Core Positioning

Auralis Digital helps local businesses look trustworthy online and turn attention into calls, bookings, visits, and quote requests.

The current strongest positioning is:

> Professional websites for Michigan local businesses built to earn trust, clarify services, and drive calls, bookings, and quote requests.

## What Is Working

- Clear local business market need
- Live public website
- Custom domain is active: `https://www.auralisdigital.net/`
- Founder-led service model
- Affordable starting pricing framework
- Roadmap centered on revenue instead of endless redesign
- Early portfolio/demo direction already started
- Future Nova-powered service direction is identified but not overstated

## Main Gaps

- Real lead capture form is not implemented yet

- Need more niche-specific demos
- Need testimonials and proof over time
- Need clearer service/package docs
- Need a defined client funnel and quote process
- Outreach consistency will determine growth
- Future Nova-backed services need to stay staged until real capability exists

## Nova Relationship

Auralis Digital may become the first commercial surface for NovaLIS over time, but the current business should sell websites first.

Near-term:

> Auralis builds websites and improves lead capture.

Later:

> Auralis websites can become Nova-ready through structured forms, business profiles, lead summaries, draft replies, and metrics reports.

Important boundary:

> Do not market Nova-backed services as live customer products until the runtime capability, approval flow, support model, and trust visibility are actually ready.

## Final Verdict

The next growth stage is not more planning. It is proof, conversations, consistency, and closed deals.

Auralis Digital is positioned well enough to start serious outreach, but the repo and website should prioritize execution hygiene:

- real form backend;
- clear packages;
- niche demos;
- outreach pipeline;
- testimonials;
- staged Nova-ready structure.
