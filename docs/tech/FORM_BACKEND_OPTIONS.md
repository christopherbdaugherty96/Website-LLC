# Form Backend Options

Status: implementation guide

The current contact flow should move beyond mailto links into a real lead capture system.

---

## What a Real Form Should Do

- Send inquiry notifications
- Store leads safely
- Reduce spam
- Work on mobile
- Confirm successful submission
- Route leads to correct email
- Optionally log to spreadsheet/CRM

---

## Recommended Options

## 1. Formspree

Good for quick setup and static websites.

Best for:
- simple lead forms
- fast launch
- low maintenance

## 2. Netlify Forms

Good when hosted on Netlify.

Best for:
- simple static sites
- GitHub + Netlify workflow

## 3. Basin

Good dedicated form handling.

Best for:
- clean standalone form service
- spam protection

## 4. Google Forms

Good temporary low-cost option.

Best for:
- internal intake
- simple request capture

## 5. Custom Backend Endpoint

Best for advanced workflows.

Examples:
- CRM integration
- auto-replies
- lead scoring
- multi-step workflows
- custom dashboards

---

## Default Recommendation

Use Formspree or Netlify Forms first. Keep it simple unless the client truly needs custom workflows.

---

## Internal Rule

The best form system is the one that reliably captures leads and is easy to maintain.
