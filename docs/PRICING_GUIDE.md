# Pricing Guide

## Purpose
Use clear value-based language when discussing pricing.

## Core Offers

### Website Refresh — Starting at $250
Best for businesses with an existing site that needs cleaner wording, better trust, and easier customer action.

### Starter Website — Starting at $500
Best for businesses that need a credible online presence with clear services and contact paths.

### Standard Website — Starting at $1,000
Best for businesses that need a stronger brand presence, multiple sections/pages, and a more polished conversion flow.

### Custom Quote
For unique scopes, advanced features, or larger builds.

## Rule
Sell outcomes and clarity, not just pages.
